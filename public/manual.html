How To Use

Follow these simple steps to start using NERIST Hub effectively.

How to Post in Marketplace

1

Login to Your Account

Click on "Login" button at top right. Use your college Registration no. (ex-225/xxx) to register/login.

2

Navigate to Marketplace

From the homepage menu, click on "Marketplace" or use Quick Actions section.

3

Click "Add New Post"

Look for the blue button with plus icon.

4

Fill the Form

Add title, description, price, category, and upload photos. Be clear and honest about the item condition.

5

Submit

Click "Post Item". Your listing will be live instantly. You can delete it anytime from your profile.

How to Report a Lost Item

1

Go to Lost & Found

Access from main navigation menu or homepage.

2

Click "Lost Item"

Choose this option if you've lost something.

3

Describe Your Item

Item name, color, location lost, date, and any identifying features. Upload photo if available.

4

Contact Info

Your contact details will be shown to finders.

How to Find a Service

1

Open Services Section

Click on "Services" from the dropdown menu or homepage.

2

Select Category

Choose from Printing, Washing Machine, Bike/Scooty rental, Drawing, etc.

3

View Providers

See list of service providers with price range, and location.

4

Contact Directly

Email the provider using the provided Email.

Quick Tips & Best Practices

Add Clear Photos

Listings with clear photos usually get more attention. A few good pictures can help others understand your item better.

Update Status

Mark items as "Sold" or "Found" immediately after deal is done to avoid confusion.

Dark Mode Available

Toggle theme using the sun/moon icon. Saves battery on OLED screens and reduces eye strain.

Enable Notifications

Get instant alerts when someone post or finds lost item.
